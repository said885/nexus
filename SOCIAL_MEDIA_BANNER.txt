NEXUS (Post-Quantum Messaging Interface)

Overview:
NEXUS is a next-generation messaging protocol built for the quantum era. It provides end-to-end encryption using NIST-standardized Post-Quantum KEM and digital signatures, ensuring complete confidentiality even in the future when quantum computers arrive.

Features:
- Hybrid PQ-Cryptography (Kyber1024 + X25519)
- PQ-Digital Signatures (Dilithium5 + Ed25519)
- 100% Rust memory-safe implementation
- Zero-Knowledge Relay (metadata-preserving)
- Sealed Sender technology
- Verified with zero compiler warnings
